Day 1: Chest & Back

Flat Bench Press – 4 sets x 8–12 reps

Focus on keeping your back flat and engaging your chest muscles.

Incline Bench Press – 4 sets x 8–12 reps

Target the upper chest by maintaining a proper incline angle.

Dumbbell Fly – 3 sets x 10–12 reps

Keep a slight bend in your elbows and squeeze your chest at the top.
